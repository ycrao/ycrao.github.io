About TinyMe

A tiny php framework based on flight and medoo. TinyMe using third-party components, you can get help from their offical website.

Its kernel based on mikecao/flight , official website : http://flightphp.com/ , https://github.com/mikecao/flight .

DB based on catfan/medoo , official website : https://github.com/catfan/medoo , http://medoo.in/doc .
